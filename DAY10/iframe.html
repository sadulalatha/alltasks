VEG ITEMS

Sambar

Sambar is a lentil-based vegetable stew, cooked with pigeon pea and tamarind broth. It originates in South Indian cuisine and is popular in large parts of India and beyond.

Additional Information
Bhindi Fry

Bhindi Fry (also called okra fry, bhendi fry, bhindi masala or bharwan bhindi) is stir-fried okra (ladies' finger) that is slit and stuffed with spice mix such as garam masala and other locally available ground spices.

Additional Information
Muttar Paneer

Mattar paneer, also known as matar paneer, muttar paneer, and mutter paneer, is a modern restaurant-style and vegetarian punjabi dish[4][5] consisting of peas and paneer in a tomato-based sauce, spiced with garam masala.

Additional Information
Palak Paneer

Palak paneer or palak chhena is an Indian dish consisting of chhena or paneer in a thick paste made from pureed spinach, called palak in Hindi, Marathi, Gujarati, and other Indian languages.

Additional Information

NONVEG ITEMS

Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish, mainly popular in South Asia. It is made with rice, some type of meat (chicken, goat, lamb, beef, prawn, or fish) and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables or paneer for the meat. Sometimes eggs and/or potatoes are also added.

Additional Information
Chiken Curry

Chicken curry or curry/curried chicken is a South Asian dish originating from the Indian subcontinent. It is common in the cuisine of the Indian subcontinent, Caribbean, Southeast Asia, Great Britain, and Kenya.

Additional Information
Mutton Curry

Mutton curry (also referred to as kosha mangsho, lamb curry, or goat curry) is a dish that is prepared from goat meat (or sometimes lamb meat) and vegetables. The dish is found in different variations across all states, countries and regions of the Indian subcontinent and the Caribbean.

Additional Information
Fish

Many species of fish are caught by humans and consumed as food in virtually all regions around the world. Fish has been an important dietary source of protein and other nutrients.
